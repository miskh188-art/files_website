Billing questions

What to know before you pay

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan lets you sell, add unlimited products, run a loyalty program, and view basic analytics at no cost — with no time limit and no card required.

How does the free trial work?

Paid add-ons and the Standard and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel before then at no cost.

Can I change plans later?

Anytime. Upgrade, downgrade, or turn individual add-ons on and off from your account. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Do I pay per device?

Paid plans are priced per active register. Add or remove registers as your business changes, and your bill adjusts accordingly.

What payment methods do you accept?

Major credit and debit cards. These details are placeholders — set your real terms and accepted methods before going live.
