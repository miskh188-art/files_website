What works with SwiftPOS

Receipt printers

Thermal printers over USB, Bluetooth, or wifi. Print receipts and kitchen tickets in a snap.

Barcode scanners

1D and 2D scanners for fast, accurate checkout, stock takes, and loyalty cards.

Cash drawers

Auto-opening drawers that trigger on cash sales and stay locked otherwise.

Card readers

Integrated readers from leading processors so card sales flow straight into SwiftPOS.

Phones & tablets

Run the app on iOS or Android devices you already have — no proprietary terminal required.

Stands & displays

Tablet stands and customer-facing displays to complete a tidy, professional counter.
