Good to know

Questions, answered

Is SwiftPOS really free?

Yes. The SwiftPOS app is free to use — add items, make sales, and view basic analytics at no cost. Optional add-ons like advanced inventory and employee management are available on paid plans, each with a free trial.

Does it work offline?

It does. You can keep ringing up sales without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically once you're back online. A few features, like new customer sign-ups, need a connection.

What hardware can I use?

SwiftPOS connects to receipt printers, barcode scanners, cash drawers, and card readers. See the hardware page for the full supported list and recommended kits.

How do I get started?

Download the app on iOS or Android, create a free account, add a few items, and make your first sale. Most shops are up and running the same day.
