Features

Everything you need to run the day

One connected system for selling, stocking, analyzing, and keeping customers close. Here's what's inside SwiftPOS.

Point of sale

A checkout that keeps up with the rush

Built for speed at the counter, on the floor, or at a pop-up. Add items by tap or scan, take any payment, and finish the sale fast.

  • Add items by tap, search, or barcode scan
  • Apply discounts and process refunds
  • Open tickets, split bills, and park orders
  • Print or email receipts

Payments

Take payment however they want to pay

Cash, card, or mobile wallet — SwiftPOS handles it. Connect an integrated card reader to skip manual entry and reduce errors.

  • Integrated card readers in 30+ regions
  • Cash, card, and mobile wallets
  • Split and partial payments

Inventory

Stock that counts itself

Every sale updates your counts. Reorder before you run dry, manage suppliers, and keep shelves stocked across locations.

  • Real-time stock levels and low-stock alerts
  • Purchase orders and stock receiving
  • Transfers between stores
  • Print barcode labels

Sales analytics

Numbers you can act on

Clear dashboards show how the business is doing in real time, from any device. Spot trends early and double down on what sells.

  • Revenue, profit, and average sale
  • Top items, categories, and hours
  • Full sales history and spreadsheet export

Employee management

A clearer view of your team

Track who sold what, manage clock-ins, and set access levels so everyone sees just what they need.

  • Sales by employee and top performers
  • Automatic clock-in and worked hours
  • Role-based access controls

Customer loyalty

Turn first-timers into regulars

Build a customer base, remember preferences, and reward repeat visits with a built-in points program.

  • Points-based loyalty program
  • Purchase history and customer notes
  • Identify customers by phone or barcode

Multi-store

From one counter to many

Manage every location from a single account. Compare performance and keep items, staff, and customers in sync.

  • One account across all stores
  • Compare location performance
  • Shared items, staff, and customers
